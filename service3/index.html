Hazardous Area Consultancy Services

Sangmyung engineering’s team of consultants and certification engineers have the experience and knowledge to support our customers through the certification process for equipment to be used in Hazardous Areas.

From concept design through to maintenance, Sangmyung engineering works with you to reduce the risks and accelerate your entry into global markets.

Our consultancy services include:

  • Equipment assessment for use in potentially explosive atmospheres and advice on suitable protection methods, and any re-design requirements.
  • Verification and testing of equipment to ensure compliance with applicable standards, such as IECEx, ATEX, NFPA, CSA, etc Preparation of required documentation for certification and compliance.
  • Management of our customer’s certification projects through Notified Bodies, reducing complexity and time.
  • Our Consultation and Certification team has worked within the chemical, pharmaceutical, biotech, industrial manufacturing, and marine industries, achieving Hazardous Area certifications efficiently and cost-effectively.
